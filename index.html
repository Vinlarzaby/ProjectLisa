What Are We Making About Our Devices

Our robotic makeup application system is designed to give our users an exciting, speedy and very precise stylistic output of their choosing. With this design, our aim is to invigorate the cosmetic application industry through an innovative approach in which the advancements of robotics are utilized for the benefit of individuals that deal with facial cosmetics, or even the average individual at home. Our product will offer a flexible selection of makeup design choices in addition to custom user-created modifications, which will be applied in a manner that saves time through both the speed of application and the consistency of perfection. The computer vision capabilities of our system potentially spark robotic apartment to the cosmetic industry.
